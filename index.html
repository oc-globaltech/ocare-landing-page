The platform

One secure space for every voice in a student's life.

OCare unites messaging, mood analytics, emergency response, and anonymous support — built to the privacy and governance standards education requires.

  • Anonymous emotional outlet

    A judgement-free diary & check-in where students can share feelings without fear.

  • Secure parent‑teacher‑student chat

    End-to-end encrypted threads, role-aware routing, full audit trail for schools.

  • SOS emergency alerts

    One-tap crisis signal with geolocation, reaches counsellors in under 3 seconds.

  • Admin monitoring dashboard

    School-wide vitals for safeguarding leads: mood, attendance, tickets, alerts.

  • Mood analytics

    Cohort-level trends & anomaly detection — never individual surveillance.

  • Role-based access

    Students, parents, teachers, counsellors & admins each see exactly what they should.
