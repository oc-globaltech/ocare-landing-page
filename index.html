The problem

Today's schools run on duct tape and good intentions.

Emotional health slips through the cracks. Messages get lost across five apps. Emergencies take too long. OCare replaces all of it.

Emotional issues go unnoticed

1 in 5 students silently struggles. Without a safe, regular pulse-check, warning signs are invisible until crisis.

Unnoticed until crisis 68%

Communication scattered across apps

WhatsApp, email, printed notices, diary pages. Parents miss updates, teachers miss context, counsellors miss signals.

Channels per school 6 AVG

Slow emergency response

When a student needs help, a chain of phone calls and paperwork costs precious minutes. SOS should be one tap.

Avg. response time 22 MIN

No central monitoring system

Administrators lack a live, school-wide view of well-being, engagement, and risk — making early intervention impossible.

The platform

One secure space for every voice in a student's life.

OCare unites messaging, mood analytics, emergency response, and anonymous support — built to the privacy and governance standards education requires.

  • Anonymous emotional outlet

    A judgement-free diary & check-in where students can share feelings without fear.

  • SOS emergency alerts

    One-tap crisis signal with geolocation, reaches counsellors in under 3 seconds.

  • Admin monitoring dashboard

    School-wide vitals for safeguarding leads: mood, attendance, tickets, alerts.

  • Mood analytics

    Cohort-level trends & anomaly detection — never individual surveillance.

  • Role-based access

    Students, parents, teachers, counsellors & admins each see exactly what they should.
